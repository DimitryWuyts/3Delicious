Dinner's ready!

If you've ever attempted to make grandma's fine shark fin soup, you know it takes ages to make a batch so you probably failed because, if your grandma made shark fin soup,that means you're asian therefore you're an instant disgrace to your family especially when you gave up halfway making it.
With 3Delicious our printers can give you your shark fins in a whip and a snip AND put it in grandma's soup for your and your family's enjoyment.

The same goes for dessert

Do you know what it takes to make a chocolate Oreo cake? Do you? You need milk, flour, eggs, butter, sugar, chocolate, Oreo's, whisks, bowls, a complete darn stove including an oven and, well, a whole kitchen basically. We don't care if you live in a mansion or in a coffin sized apartment. We guarantee you a splendid home chef experience.

Print your meat while it gets cooked

Are you tired of waiting for your steak to cook? We have the solution for that. In our specialised 3Delicious printer 3000 we included a ceramic induction cooktop on which will instantly fry your meat to the exact degree you desire. Complete with an oven door so you also have the option to print and bake too.
Now that's what we call multitasking!
